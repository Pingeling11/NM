Norse Runes

The Elder Futhark was used from the second to the eighth century. It contains 24 letters.

Fehu [F]

Translation: cattle

Meaning: wealth; prosperity; creativity; nourishment

Ûruz [U,V]

Translation: aurochs

Meaning: power; speed

Thurisaz [TH]

Translation: Jötin

Meaning: protection; obstacle; reflection; rest

Ansuz [A]

Translation: Aesir

Meaning: communication; divine power; signals

Raidô [R]

Translation: journey

Meaning: change; action

Kenaz [K,C]

Translation: light

Meaning: enlightenment; divine light

Gebô [G]

Translation: gift

Meaning: spiritual gift; generosity; talent

Wunjô [W]

Translation: joy

Meaning: balance; harmony

Haglaz [H]

Translation: hail

Meaning: challenges; internal and external forces

Naudiz [N]

Translation: needs

Meaning: self-development

Îsaz [I]

Translation: ice

Meaning: inactivity; status quo; obstacles

Jêra [J]

Translation: year

Meaning: taking advantage

Îhwaz [Ï]

Translation: jew tree

Meaning: motivation; transformation

Perthrô

Translation: dice cup

Meaning: fate

Algiz [Z]

Translation: moose

Meaning: protection; defense

Sôwilô [S]

Translation: sun

Meaning: prosperity; health

Tîwaz [T]

Translation: Tyr

Meaning: heroism; victory; resposibility

Berkanan [B]

Translation: birch

Meaning: (re)birth; purification; fertility

Ehwaz [E]

Translation: horse

Meaning: cooperation; progress; change

Mannaz [M]

Translation: human

Meaning: humanity, self, ego

Laguz [L]

Translation: water

Meaning: intuition; emotions; reflection

Ingwaz [ING]

Translation: Inguz

Meaning: fertility; creativity

Dagaz [D]

Translation: day(light)

Meaning: clarity; attentiveness; enlightenment

Ôdhala [O]

Translation: property

Meaning: inheritance; ancestors
