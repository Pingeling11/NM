Norse Gods

There are two families in the Norse mythology: the Aesir and the Vanir.

This are some of the more known Gods and Goddesses.

Name God/Goddess of
Odin war, knowledge, battle
Thor lightning, thunder
Freya love, fertility, lust
Freyr abundance, peace, prosperity, sun , rain
Njord sea, nautical, sailing art
Tyr war, defense, victory
Loki trickery, deceit
Baldr the beautiful, the good

Weekdays

Monday: Mani (the Norse moon god)

Tuesday: Tyr

Wednesday: Odin

Thursday: Thor

Friday: Freya

Saturday: Saturnus (the Roman agricultural god)

Sunday: Sunna (the Norse sun goddess)
